# US Watchlist Builder
{: .no_toc }

Auto-build 4 curated watchlists from the US market (Growth, Value, Momentum, Mean-Reversion) using TradingView Screener filters. Reduce thousands of US stocks to 20-50 candidates per bucket for focused analysis.
